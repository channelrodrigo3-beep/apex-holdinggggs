🏛️ About Apex Holdings

Founded in 2018, Apex Holdings has grown from a small fintech startup to one of the most trusted names in stock market education. With over 500,000 users worldwide, we've helped aspiring traders learn the ropes without risking real capital.

Our mission is simple: democratize financial education. We believe everyone should have access to professional-grade trading tools to learn, practice, and build confidence before entering real markets.
