❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add money to my account?

Go to Dashboard, enter an amount in the Cash Management section, and click "Add Money". Funds are instantly available for trading.

Can I withdraw my virtual cash?

Yes! Use the "Withdraw" button on Dashboard. This is great for practicing cash management strategies.

Is this real money?

No, Apex Holdings is 100% simulated. It's designed for educational purposes to learn trading without financial risk.

How do I reset my password?

Go to Profile section, enter your current password and new password in the Security section, then click Update Password.

What happens if I lose all my money?

Contact the admin through the support email to reset your account back to $100,000. Or use the Add Money feature to add funds anytime.
